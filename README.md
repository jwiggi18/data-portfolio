# Data Portfolio

A collection of data analysis projects focused on biology, education, and workforce outcomes.  
This portfolio showcases skills in data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and reproducible research using Python, R, and Quarto.

🔗 **Live site:** [Data Analysis & Visualization Portfolio](https://jwiggi18.github.io/data-portfolio/)

---

## 📊 Projects

### 🦎 Collared Lizard Distribution in Oklahoma
Mapping and analyzing the distribution of collared lizards using biodiversity occurrence data.

**Focus:**
- Data acquisition from public biodiversity sources (iNaturalist) via an API
- Geospatial analysis and visualization
- Species distribution patterns

**Tools:** Python, mapping libraries, data cleaning workflows

---

### 🎓 Life Sciences PhD Career Outcomes *(in progress)*
An analysis of what jobs life sciences PhDs pursue after completing their degrees, with a focus on alignment between doctoral training and career outcomes.

**Focus:**
- Employment sector and job type
- Teaching vs research roles
- Early-career trajectories

**Data sources:**
- NSF Survey of Doctorate Recipients (SDR)
- NSF Early Career Doctorates Survey (ECDS)
- Survey of Earned Doctorates (SED)
